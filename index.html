Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to what site owners ask us most about maintenance, security, and support.

1. What's included in the free website audit?

We check your site's security, speed, and overall health, then send you a short report highlighting the biggest risks and quick wins.

2. How many update requests can I submit?

You can submit up to 5 update requests a day on our Premium and Enterprise plans, each turned around in 15 minutes or less, with no caps or extra fees.

3. Do you work with platforms other than WordPress?

Yes. While we specialize in WordPress maintenance, we also support custom PHP sites and other CMS platforms.

4. What happens if my site gets hacked?

Our team removes malware and restores your site from the most recent backup, then hardens it against the same attack happening again.

5. Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time as your site's needs change.
